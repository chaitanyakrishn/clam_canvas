FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What is the mental health tracking system?The mental health tracking system is a platform that allows users to monitor and track their mental well-being through various tools and features.
How does the chatbot development feature work?The chatbot development feature enables users to interact with a virtual assistant that provides support, guidance, and resources for mental health concerns.
What is the community support network?The community support network is a platform where users can connect with others facing similar challenges, share experiences, and offer mutual support.
How can personalized assessments help improve well-being?Personalized assessments provide users with insights into their mental health status, enabling them to identify areas for improvement and access tailored resources.
Are the resources provided on the platform evidence-based?Yes, the resources available on the platform are curated from reputable sources and are evidence-based to ensure their effectiveness in supporting mental well-being.
